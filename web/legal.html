Legal Information

Clear terms for a private portfolio tracking demo.

AssetYantra is a portfolio tracking and reporting platform. This page explains the basic legal, privacy, and usage expectations for demo and client review purposes.

Important Note

Not financial advice.

Stock prices, reports, and profit/loss views are shown for portfolio tracking and demonstration. Users should verify data before making investment decisions.

Terms Of Use

Use the platform responsibly.

The dashboard is intended for registered admins and clients only. Login credentials, OTPs, and client IDs should not be shared publicly.

Privacy

Client data stays inside the secured workspace.

Customer details, holdings, booked profit/loss, and reports are stored for dashboard functionality and admin review. Archived clients are hidden from active views.

Market Data

Live prices depend on provider availability.

Market prices may come from third-party sources and can be delayed, cached, unavailable, or different from broker/exchange execution prices.

Reports

Downloaded reports are for review meetings.

PDF/profile exports summarize portfolio values at the time of generation and should be checked against the latest market and account records.

Need Access?

Admins create customers and manage portfolio records inside the dashboard.

Use the secure login page to enter the private workspace.
