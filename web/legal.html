Privacy Policy

How We Handle Your Data

Your privacy is fundamental to how we operate. Asset Yantra collects and processes personal data solely to provide, improve, and secure the platform experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly — including your name, phone number, email address, and Client ID — when you register, contact us, or use the platform. We also collect limited usage data (page views, session timestamps) to improve platform performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to: (a) authenticate your identity and provide secure access to the dashboard; (b) send OTPs and account-related notifications; (c) deliver portfolio reports and algorithmic trading performance updates; (d) respond to enquiries and support requests.

3. Data Storage & Security

Client data is stored on secured cloud infrastructure. We employ industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access to client data is restricted to authorised Asset Yantra personnel on a need-to-know basis. We do not store financial account credentials or broker login details.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting info@assetyantra.com, subject to any legal retention obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also opt out of non-essential communications at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact our team directly.

Risk Disclosure

Understanding Investment Risk

All investments, including algorithmic trading and portfolio management, carry inherent risk. Past performance of the Asset Yantra Algo or any Asset Yantra-managed portfolio does not guarantee future returns.

Algorithmic Trading (Forex / XAU/USD)

Foreign exchange and gold (XAU/USD) markets are volatile and subject to rapid price movements driven by macroeconomic events, geopolitical factors, and central bank decisions. While the Asset Yantra Algo has demonstrated consistent weekly profitability since launch, individual weeks may result in a loss, as shown in our transparent performance data.

Portfolio Management

Equity and multi-asset portfolio management involves market risk, liquidity risk, and concentration risk. Capital invested in a managed portfolio may increase or decrease in value. Asset Yantra will endeavour to manage risk through disciplined allocation, but cannot eliminate market risk entirely.

Capital at Risk

You should not invest money you cannot afford to lose. The minimum recommended capital for the algorithmic trading service is $1,500 USD. Invest only what is within your risk tolerance and long-term financial plan.

Cookie Policy

How We Use Cookies

Asset Yantra uses a minimal set of cookies and local storage to ensure the platform functions correctly and securely. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the platform to operate and cannot be disabled. They include session authentication tokens, login state, and security flags. Without these, you cannot access the dashboard.

Performance & Analytics

We may collect anonymised usage statistics (page loads, session duration) to understand how the platform is used and where to improve. This data does not identify you personally.

No Third-Party Advertising

Asset Yantra does not use Google Ads, Meta Pixel, or any retargeting cookies. Your browsing behaviour on this platform is not shared with advertisers.

Managing Cookies

You may clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent login functionality. For questions about cookie usage, contact info@assetyantra.com.
