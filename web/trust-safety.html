Trust Framework

Four principles shape the experience.
Verified access

Role-based entry and OTP-backed login make access feel controlled.

Visible performance

Holdings show buy price, live price, and P&L together.

Separated privileges

Users stay in their own workspace while admins use restricted controls.

Recorded outcomes

Exports and server-backed data create a stronger operational trail.

Operational Safety

Structured role separation keeps oversight, moderation, and client actions in the right hands.

Admins review multiple clients and exports. Users remain within their own holdings and portfolio context.

Communication Safety

Performance is shown in a way that supports clarity first, reaction second.

Key values are grouped, directional cues stay consistent, and supporting copy reduces confusion.

Integrity Controls

Security and data decisions designed for real product use.

The trust layer now looks premium without losing practical discipline.

04Audit trail
