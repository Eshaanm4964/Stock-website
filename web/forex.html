Our Approach

Rule-based, research-driven, RBI-compliant

All international strategies at AssetYantra are designed within the regulatory framework of RBI's Liberalised Remittance Scheme (LRS) — up to USD 250,000 per financial year for Indian residents — and FEMA guidelines for NRI investors.

We do not speculate on currency movements. Instead, our framework uses macro indicators, interest rate differentials, purchasing power parity benchmarks, and cross-asset correlation data to guide structured, long-horizon international participation.
