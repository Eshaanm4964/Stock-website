FAQ

Common Questions

How does profit sharing work?

At the end of each billing cycle, 30% of the net profit generated by the algorithm on your account is shared as a performance fee. If the algo doesn't profit, you don't pay the profit-sharing component — only the annual subscription applies.

What happens after I pay?

Our onboarding team contacts you within 24 hours. You'll be guided through broker account setup, capital allocation, and the algo will be connected and active — typically within 48–72 hours of your subscription.

What is the minimum capital required?

The recommended minimum is $1,500 USD for meaningful results. However, the algorithm can be configured for higher capital accounts (like $5,000 or above) with proportionally stronger weekly performance.

Can I track my performance?

Yes. You get access to a live performance dashboard showing every trade, weekly results, win rates, and cumulative profit — transparent and updated in real time.

Can I withdraw my funds anytime?

Your funds are always in your own brokerage account — we never hold your money. Withdrawals can be made at any time directly through your broker, subject to their standard processing timelines.

I am new to Forex. Can I contact support if I have questions?

Absolutely. We offer dedicated phone support and priority access for all subscribers. Our team will walk you through everything from account setup to understanding your weekly reports.

Can I pause the algorithm if I want?

Yes, you can request a pause at any time. Simply contact our support team and we'll deactivate the algo on your account. Your subscription continues, and you can resume whenever you're ready.

Do I need to log in every day or watch the trades manually?

No. The algorithm is 100% automated. It monitors markets 24/7, places orders, and manages risk — entirely without manual intervention. You can check your performance whenever you want, but you never have to.
